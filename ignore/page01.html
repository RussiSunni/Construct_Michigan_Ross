Messaging

Once we figure out "who" our customer is we need to think about how we message them. We'll need a compelling message delivered in the right place and the key aspects to consider here are that it needs to be optimized to the target and it needs to be consistently delivered across everything that you do.

"Good" messaging needs to:

  1. Address customer needs, appeal to the target customer
  2. Differentiate from the competition
  3. Clearly describe the benefits of your product
